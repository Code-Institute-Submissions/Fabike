WHO WE ARE

FABIKE project started in 2011. The founder of the company and driving force behind the FABIKE brand is Fabio Putzolu. He comes from a family with a long cycling tradition, and grew up around passionate cyclists. Fabio's main inspiration for the FABIKE project is his uncle Antonino, who was already spreading a strong passion for cycling in his native Sardinia - Italy, in the early 1950s.

For many years Fabio studied and worked in design and advertising, and is thrilled to combine so many of his passions under one roof. He founded FABIKE Design during his time spent in Prague, Czech republic, where the company is now based.

As the project grew and developed, Jake and Alexey joined, bringing a wealth of diverse experience to the team. In August 2013, FABIKE was officially presented to the public at Eurobike, the largest cycling trade show in the world. We have worked hard to passionately improve our products day after day since then.

Today, our bikes are riding in more than 20 countries worldwide, spreading the word, and showing people what a contemporary bicycle could be.
